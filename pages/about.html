All About BookBot!

Welcome to BookBot!

BookBot is specifically designed to find free audiobooks. If you already know what you're looking for, just search the title and navigate to the linked youtube audiobook. If you're not sure what you're looking for, you can search by authors or genres! Each book includes a summary so you can get a glimpse of what you'd be in for.

All summaries come from Amazon and Wikipedia.

Click the search icon at the top and log in to get started.

Happy listening!
